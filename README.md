## 🚀 Propulsion Modules: Engineering the Future of Motion

Our goal is to **decarbonize movement** across air, sea, and land. We go beyond incremental gains and develop new propulsion paradigms — modular, scalable, and planet-positive.

### 🔧 Focus Areas
- **Electric Drives**
- **Hydrogen Propulsion**
- **Hybrid Architectures**

### 🧪 Core Technologies
- ⚡ **Solid-State Battery Systems**  
  High-density, fast-charging, and inherently safe for all mobility classes.

- 💧 **Green Hydrogen Fuel Cells**  
  High-efficiency PEM stacks running on clean H₂, ideal for long-haul and marine.

- 🧲 **Switched Reluctance Motors (SRMs)**  
  Rare-earth-free, cost-efficient, and robust against harsh duty cycles.

- ♻️ **Kinetic Energy Recovery Systems (KERS)**  
  Ultra-efficient regenerative systems with 90%+ recovery rates for vehicles and industry.

## Key Features of Q-GREENTECH Structure:

### 1. **ATA Chapter Coverage** (Aircraft Systems)
- **Propulsion**: ATA 28, 71, 72, 73
- **Energy**: ATA 24, 42
- **Recycling**: ATA 20, 51, plus end-of-life considerations
- **Systems**: ATA 21, 36, 38, 49

### 2. **SSA Chapter Coverage** (Spacecraft Systems)
- **Propulsion**: SSA 03 (Green propellants, electric propulsion)
- **Energy**: SSA 07 (Solar, nuclear alternatives)
- **Recycling**: SSA 02 (On-orbit recycling)
- **Systems**: SSA 04, 08 (Life support, thermal management)

### 3. **Cross-Cutting Themes**
- Alternative fuels (SAF, hydrogen)
- Electrification and hybridization
- Circular economy and recycling
- Emissions reduction
- Energy efficiency
- Sustainable materials

### 4. **Integration Points**
- Links to Q-AIR for aircraft implementation
- Links to Q-SPACE for spacecraft implementation
- Feeds into Q-DATAGOV for compliance tracking
- Uses Q-HPC for simulations and analysis
- Contributes to Q-SCIRES for research

### 5. **Documentation Structure**
- Technical specifications per ATA/SSA chapter
- Sustainability metrics and tracking
- Certification pathways
- Training materials
- Analysis tools

This structure ensures Q-GREENTECH covers all green technology aspects across the relevant ATA/SSA chapters while maintaining clear interfaces with other GAIA-QAO domains.
